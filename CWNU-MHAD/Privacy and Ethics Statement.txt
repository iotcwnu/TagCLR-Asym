The CWNU-MHAD dataset contains RGB video data of volunteers performing human activities. The videos may include personally identifiable information such as the volunteers’ faces, physical appearance, and movement patterns. Therefore, all users must strictly adhere to research ethics, privacy protection principles, and relevant laws and regulations when downloading, accessing, and using this dataset.
This dataset is permitted solely for academic research, teaching, algorithm validation, and non-commercial research related to human activity recognition. It is strictly prohibited to use this dataset for any purpose unrelated to human activity recognition, including but not limited to facial recognition, identity verification, personal identity matching, person tracking, behavioral profiling, privacy inference, commercial surveillance, or any other use that may infringe upon the privacy and rights of the volunteers.
Without written permission from the dataset publisher, users may not disseminate, resell, publicly release, or redistribute the raw video data or its still frames to third parties. If data samples are displayed in papers, reports, presentations, code repositories, or other public materials, volunteers’ faces and other personally identifiable information must be blurred, obscured, or anonymized to prevent the disclosure of personal privacy.
Users must not attempt to recover, infer, label, or disclose the true identity of volunteers, nor may they perform associative analysis by linking this dataset with other data sources containing personally identifiable information. Users shall bear full responsibility for any privacy breaches, ethical risks, or legal liabilities resulting from the improper use, dissemination, or processing of this dataset.
By downloading or using this dataset, users acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agree to comply with the above privacy and ethics statement.
